Could you explain the difference between RESTful APIs and GraphQL, and when you might choose one over the other?

You Just now

RESTful APIs and GraphQL are both approaches for building APIs, but they have significant differences:

RESTful APIs use multiple endpoints for different resources and standard HTTP methods. They typically return fixed data structures and may require multiple requests to fetch related data.

GraphQL, on the other hand, uses a single endpoint and allows clients to request exactly the data they need in a single query. This reduces over-fetching and under-fetching of data.

I would choose REST for simple APIs with well-defined resource types and when caching is important. GraphQL works better for complex applications with interconnected data and when bandwidth efficiency matters, such as mobile apps.
